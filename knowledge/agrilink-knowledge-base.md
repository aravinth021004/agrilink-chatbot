# AgriLink - Complete Platform Knowledge Base

## About AgriLink

AgriLink is a social commerce platform that connects farmers directly with consumers. The platform eliminates middlemen so farmers receive fair prices for their produce. Traditionally, farmers only receive 25-35% of retail prices due to intermediaries. AgriLink solves this through a subscription-based model (₹199/month for farmers) instead of transaction commissions.

### Mission
To empower farmers by providing direct access to consumers, enabling fair pricing and sustainable agriculture.

### Key Statistics
- 5,000+ farmers on the platform
- 50,000+ products listed
- 100,000+ happy customers
- Available in 50+ cities across India

### Core Values
- **Direct Connection**: Removing middlemen from the supply chain
- **Sustainability**: Promoting eco-friendly farming practices
- **Farmer Empowerment**: Giving farmers tools and market access
- **Community First**: Building a community around local food

## User Roles

### Customer (Default Role)
Every new user starts as a Customer. Customers can:
- Browse the product feed (social media style layout)
- Search and filter products by category, location, and price
- Like, comment on, and share product posts
- Follow their favorite farmers
- Add products to cart and place orders
- Choose delivery options: Home Delivery, Farm Pickup, or Meetup Point
- Track order status with a timeline
- Rate and review farmers (1-5 stars, only after completing a purchase)
- Send direct messages to farmers
- Manage a wishlist of favorite products
- Upgrade to Farmer role by subscribing

### Farmer (Subscription Required)
Farmers have all Customer features plus:
- Create product posts with images/videos, price, quantity, and descriptions
- Set delivery options and delivery radius (in kilometers)
- Set delivery fees
- Manage inventory and product status
- Accept or reject incoming orders
- Update order status through the lifecycle: Pending → Confirmed → Packed → Out for Delivery → Delivered
- Upload delivery proof (optional)
- View analytics: sales, views, revenue
- Chat with customers about orders
- Create custom product categories

**Important**: Farmers must have an active subscription to list new products. When a subscription expires, the farmer is downgraded to Customer, but existing posts remain visible.

### Admin
Admins manage the platform:
- User management: suspend, activate, or manage user accounts
- Content moderation: review reported posts, remove or dismiss reports
- Subscription management: approve subscriptions, handle issues
- Platform analytics: total users, revenue, active subscriptions, engagement metrics
- Configure platform settings (e.g., UPI payment details)

## How to Get Started

### Creating an Account
1. Click "Sign Up" on the homepage or navigation bar
2. Enter your full name, email address, and phone number
3. Create a password (must be strong)
4. You will receive an OTP (One-Time Password) for email/phone verification
5. Enter the OTP to verify your account
6. Set up your profile with a photo and bio (optional)
7. You're ready to start browsing!

### Browsing Products
- The **Feed** page shows products in a social media style layout (similar to Instagram)
- Products are ranked by a feed algorithm considering:
  - Recency (posts from last 48 hours are prioritized)
  - Engagement (likes, comments, shares)
  - Relationship (following the farmer, past orders, same location)
- Use infinite scroll to see more products (20 per page)
- Use the **Search** page to filter by category, location, or price range
- Use the **Categories** page to browse by product type

### Following Farmers
- Visit a farmer's profile and click "Follow"
- Following a farmer boosts their posts in your feed
- You'll see their new products more prominently

## Shopping & Ordering

### Adding to Cart
1. Browse products on the feed or search page
2. Click "Add to Cart" on any product
3. Select quantity and delivery option
4. Items are grouped by farmer in the cart

### Cart Management
- View your cart from the cart icon in the navigation bar
- Items are organized by farmer for easy checkout
- Update quantities or remove items
- See subtotal per farmer plus delivery fees
- The grand total includes all items and delivery charges

### Delivery Options
Each farmer may offer one or more delivery options:
1. **Home Delivery**: Products delivered to your doorstep. A delivery fee and delivery radius apply.
2. **Farm Pickup**: Visit the farmer's location to collect your order (usually free).
3. **Meetup Point**: Meet the farmer at a pre-arranged location.

### Checkout Process
1. Review your cart (items grouped by farmer)
2. Ensure delivery address is set (for Home Delivery) or pickup details are confirmed
3. Choose payment method:
   - **UPI**: Scan QR code or enter UPI ID, then provide your 12-digit UPI transaction reference ID
   - **Cash on Delivery (COD)**: Pay when you receive the order
4. A separate order is created for each farmer in your cart
5. You'll receive order confirmation

### Payment Methods
- **UPI**: Digital payment through any UPI-enabled app (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.)
- **Cash on Delivery (COD)**: Pay in cash when the order is delivered
- All payments are processed securely

## Order Lifecycle

### Order Status Flow
1. **Pending**: Order placed, waiting for farmer to respond
2. **Confirmed**: Farmer accepted the order
3. **Packed**: Products are packed and ready
4. **Out for Delivery**: Order is on its way
5. **Delivered**: Order has been delivered successfully
6. **Cancelled**: Order was cancelled (by customer or farmer)
7. **Disputed**: There's an issue that needs resolution

### For Customers
- View all orders in the "Orders" section
- Track each order's status with a visual timeline
- Chat with the farmer about your order
- Mark order as "Received" when delivered
- Rate the farmer after delivery (1-5 stars with optional review)
- Cancel an order before it's confirmed by the farmer

### For Farmers
- View incoming orders in the Dashboard
- Accept or reject orders
- Update order status as it progresses
- Upload delivery proof (optional photo)
- Chat with the customer about the order

### Order Cancellation
- Customers can cancel orders **before** the farmer confirms them
- Once confirmed, contact the farmer directly through messages to request cancellation
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days

## Subscription System (For Farmers)

### Why Subscribe?
To sell products on AgriLink, you need an active farmer subscription. This is how we keep the platform free of transaction commissions — a small monthly fee instead.

### Subscription Plans
AgriLink offers tiered plans (prices start at ₹199/month):
- Plans include features like product listing, analytics, and priority support
- Duration varies (monthly, quarterly, yearly)
- Some plans are marked as "Most Popular"

### How to Subscribe
1. Go to the **Subscription** page
2. Choose a plan that suits your needs
3. Pay via UPI: Scan the QR code with any UPI app
4. Enter the 12-digit UPI reference ID from your transaction
5. Submit for admin approval
6. The admin will verify your payment and activate your subscription

### Subscription Status
- **Active**: You can list products and manage orders
- **Pending**: Payment submitted, awaiting admin verification
- **Expired**: Subscription ended; you'll be downgraded to Customer
- **Cancelled**: Subscription was cancelled

### Subscription Expiry
- Reminders are sent 7 days before expiry
- On expiry, the account is automatically downgraded to Customer
- Existing product posts remain visible but you cannot create new ones
- Renew anytime to regain farmer capabilities

### Common Questions Quick Answers

**Q: How do I create an account?**
A: Click "Sign Up", enter your email or phone number, verify with OTP, and set up your profile.

**Q: How do I find products near me?**
A: Use the Search page with location filters, or browse the Feed which prioritizes local products.

**Q: How do I place an order?**
A: Add items to cart, choose delivery option, enter address if needed, and complete payment.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: UPI (all UPI apps) and Cash on Delivery (COD).

**Q: How does delivery work?**
A: Farmers offer Home Delivery, Farm Pickup, or Meetup Point options. Fees and radius vary by farmer.

**Q: Can I cancel my order?**
A: Yes, before the farmer confirms it. After confirmation, contact the farmer to request cancellation. Refunds processed in 5-7 business days.

**Q: How do I become a seller/farmer?**
A: Sign up as a customer, then go to the Subscription page and subscribe to a farmer plan.

**Q: How do I receive payments as a farmer?**
A: Payments are credited to your registered bank account within 2-3 business days after delivery.

**Q: How do I list products?**
A: Go to Dashboard → Add Product → Upload photos → Fill in details → Set delivery options → Publish.

**Q: What happens when my subscription expires?**
A: You're downgraded to a Customer. Existing posts remain visible, but you can't create new ones.

**Q: How do I change my language?**
A: Go to Settings or use the language selector in the navigation bar. Available: English, Hindi, Tamil.

**Q: How do I report an issue?**
A: Use the Report button on any product, user, or comment. Admins will review and take action.
